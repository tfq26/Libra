# Libra

Libra is a web application for managing personal finances. It features a user-friendly interface and powerful analytics capabilities.

## Features

- **Expense Tracking**: Keep track of your expenses with ease. Add new expenses, categorize them, and generate detailed reports.

- **Budgeting**: Create and manage your budgets with customizable categories and alerts.

- **Income Tracking**: Monitor your income streams and plan your finances accordingly.

- **Analytics**: Get insights into your spending habits with detailed charts and graphs.

- **Offline Support**: Access your finances offline with the PWA (Progressive Web App) feature.

- **Multi-User Support**: Share your finances with family and friends by adding multiple users to your account.

- **Secure**: All your data is securely stored and encrypted.
